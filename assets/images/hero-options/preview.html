Hero Background Options

Option 1 — Solid Navy

#2e2d4d
Clean, minimal. The navy is directly from the brand palette. Screen blend works perfectly — black pixels in the video become transparent, coloured pixels glow. Very Pentagram / Made Thought feel.

Option 2 — Navy → Black Gradient

#2e2d4d → #000
Adds vertical depth — navy at top (where the logo sits) fading to black at bottom. Creates a sense of infinite space. Still fully on-brand.

Option 3 — Navy + Pink Centre Glow

#2e2d4d + #e75a7c
Subtle radial pink warmth at centre where the animation plays, fading to deeper navy at edges. Brings the brand's "bubbly, passionate" energy into the hero while keeping contrast high.

Option 4 — Abstract Dark Texture

Navy + organic noise
Organic, smoky texture using navy and hints of pink. More visual interest than a flat colour, with a fashion-editorial quality. Still dark enough for clean blending.

Option 5 — Darkened Original Photo

Keeps the original behind-the-scenes photography but dramatically darkened and tinted navy. Adds story context while solving the colour-blending issue.
